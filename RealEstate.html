Real Estate

One of the simplest, yet most effective and common methods of investing is throguh real estate. Anyone who owns (not rents) a house or an appartment is considered to be invested in real estate.

Real estate is considered a very safe investment as it is rare for something to happen to a house that would drastically decrease its value. The value of real estate also increases with time. The rate of this increase, however, is determined by many factors, one of the primary ones being location.
For example, an appartment in Los Angles or in New York will see its value grow more than a house in the middle of Kentucky as there is much more interest in real estate in those two growing cities.

While housing is usually safe, there are things that can cause the prices of houses to decrease drastically. For example, the 2008 housing crisis caused the values of homes to decrease immensely due to less people wanting to buy new homes. A similar thing is happening during the COVID-19 pandemic where people want to buy houses instead of appartments in order to have more breathing room, causing apartment prices to fall.

Overall, real estate is a safe investment that provides utility in the form of a residence to the investor. It is a safe, useful, and physical investment that will no doubt give an investor great returns in the future.
