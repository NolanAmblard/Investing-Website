Fundamental Analysis

Fundamental Analysis is trying to figure out the intrinsic value (what a company is really worth) of a company using any means. You could use earning reports, investor letters, or even market sentiment to determine this as almost everything plays a role into a companies value. Many great investors including the likes of Warren Buffet and Charlie Munger used fundamental analysis to grow their net worth. Due to its many applications, it is very versatile and can be used on almost any company. It also accounts for what is happening around the world, something that technical analysis users often fail to do.
