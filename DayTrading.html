Day Trading

Day trading consists of buying and selling stocks and options on small time intervals. This is one of the riskiest forms of investing and tends to make people lose money. 80% of day traders lose money. Day trading tends to attract many new and younger investors due to its promise of quick and easy cash, but sadly many people lose money. Even though many people lose money, a small portion are able to make really good money. Day trading requires a good understanding of technical and fundamental analysis in order to predict the future direction of a ticker. While it is easy to say that a stock will most likely be higher in 20-30 years, it is much harder to predict where a stock will be in 1-5 days.
